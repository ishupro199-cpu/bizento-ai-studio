# Pixalera — Product Photography Tool
## Complete AI Model Training Document + Generation Prompts v1.0

---

## WHAT THIS TOOL DOES (vs Catalog Generation)

| | Catalog Generation | Product Photography |
|---|---|---|
| Goal | Multiple views for listing | One hero artistic shot |
| Style | Clean, functional, platform-ready | Cinematic, editorial, premium |
| Output | 3–12 images, all angles | 1–4 images, maximum impact |
| Background | Mostly white/neutral | Styled, textured, scene-based |
| Feel | Product documentation | Brand story / desire creation |
| Used for | Amazon/Flipkart listing | Instagram, website, ads, brand |

This tool is for sellers and brands who want images that make people WANT the product — not just see it.

## PART 1: Product + Aesthetic Analysis

### What to extract from the uploaded image

Run this BEFORE showing any style options. This determines which styles to suggest prominently.

```
PHOTOGRAPHY ANALYSIS SCHEMA:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
product_name:            [specific]
product_category:        [Electronics / Apparel / Beauty / Food & Beverage /
                          Home Decor / Jewelry / Sports / Stationery / Other]
product_subcategory:     [e.g. Perfume Bottle / Leather Wallet / Coffee Mug]
primary_color:           [exact color name]
material_feel:           [Soft / Hard / Smooth / Rough / Glossy / Matte / Natural]
size_class:              [Tiny (ring, lipstick) / Small (mug, phone) /
                          Medium (bag, sneaker) / Large (appliance, furniture)]
premium_budget_signal:   [Luxury / Mid-range / Budget / Cannot tell]
target_audience:         [Women / Men / Unisex / Kids / Professional / Lifestyle]
product_complexity:      [Simple (one solid object) / Complex (multiple parts/colors)]
has_text_or_logo:        [yes/no]
background_in_photo:     [white / cluttered / outdoor / colored / dark]
image_quality:           [Good / Poor / Blurry / Badly lit]
```

### Auto-suggest best styles based on analysis

After analysis, suggest the top 2–3 styles with a reason:

```
STYLE SUGGESTION LOGIC:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Beauty / Skincare / Perfume    → Suggest: Dark Luxury, Minimalist, Studio
Food & Beverage                → Suggest: Lifestyle (morning/kitchen), Flat Lay, Studio
Jewelry / Watches              → Suggest: Dark Luxury, Minimalist, Outdoor (golden hour)
Apparel / Shoes / Bags         → Suggest: Lifestyle, Flat Lay, Outdoor
Electronics / Gadgets          → Suggest: Dark Luxury, Studio, Minimalist
Home Decor / Candles           → Suggest: Lifestyle (interior), Flat Lay, Minimalist
Sports / Fitness               → Suggest: Outdoor, Lifestyle, Studio
Stationery / Books             → Suggest: Flat Lay, Lifestyle (desk), Minimalist
Toys / Kids                    → Suggest: Lifestyle (playful), Flat Lay, Studio
Premium brand signal           → Always suggest Dark Luxury as top option
Budget brand signal            → Suggest Lifestyle and Flat Lay first
```

Show suggestions as clickable style cards with a 1-line reason:
> "Dark Luxury recommended — perfume bottles look most premium with dark backgrounds and rim lighting."

## PART 3: Prompt Engineering — The Core

### Universal quality suffix (append to EVERY prompt)

```
[QUALITY_SUFFIX] =
"hyperrealistic commercial product photography, 
photorealistic not illustrated, 
physically accurate materials and lighting, 
ultra sharp focus on product, 
8K resolution, DSLR quality lens rendering, 
no AI artifacts, no distortion, 
accurate color matching to original product, 
professional retouching quality output"
```

### Universal negative prompt (use with every generation)

```
[NEGATIVE_PROMPT] =
"illustration, cartoon, painting, 3D render look, toy-like,
blurry product, wrong product, duplicate product,
floating without surface, no shadows,
text overlay, watermarks, logos not on original product,
distorted proportions, extra limbs, extra objects,
cluttered background (unless lifestyle), 
cheap look, amateur lighting, flat lighting,
overexposed, underexposed, blown-out whites"
```

## PART 4: Complete Prompts — All 6 Shot Styles

Use `[PRODUCT]` = product name, `[COLOR]` = primary color, `[MATERIAL]` = material.

### STYLE 1: Studio Clean

**Best for:** All product categories. Default safe choice. Ecommerce + social media both.

**What it looks like:** Pure or soft white background. Product perfectly centered. Soft-box lighting from upper-left. Natural product shadow or reflection on surface. No props. Maximum product clarity.

#### Prompt — Studio White (Standard)
```
Professional studio product photography of [PRODUCT], [COLOR] [MATERIAL],
centered on pure white background (#FFFFFF),
soft-box lighting from upper-left at 45 degrees,
even diffused fill light from right eliminating harsh shadows,
subtle natural drop shadow directly below product,
product occupying 80% of frame, perfectly sharp focus throughout,
neutral white surface with slight reflection,
commercial catalog quality, no props, no background elements,
[QUALITY_SUFFIX]
```

#### Prompt — Studio Warm White (Premium feel)
```
Professional studio product photography of [PRODUCT], [COLOR] [MATERIAL],
warm ivory white background (#FAFAF5),
warm soft-box lighting at 3400K color temperature,
gentle directional light creating soft shadows on one side,
product on warm white matte surface with subtle reflection,
slightly elevated camera angle showing product top and front,
product at center-frame, premium commercial photography,
warm inviting tones, still crisp and sharp,
[QUALITY_SUFFIX]
```

#### Prompt — Studio Grey (Modern/Tech feel)
```
Professional studio product photography of [PRODUCT], [COLOR] [MATERIAL],
light neutral grey background (#E8E8E8 to #D0D0D0 subtle gradient),
directional studio lighting from upper-left, silver reflector fill from right,
sharp defined shadow on grey surface showing product solidity,
slightly off-center composition (rule of thirds),
camera at product eye-level, modern commercial photography style,
clean professional aesthetic, no props,
[QUALITY_SUFFIX]
```

**Best for:** Home products, beauty, food/beverage, apparel accessories, candles, stationery.

**What it looks like:** Product placed in a real or styled environment. 2–3 complementary props. Natural or window-light feel. Tells a story. Makes viewer imagine using the product.

Skincare / Beauty       → Bathroom shelf: marble surface + green plant leaf + soft natural light
Candle / Home Decor     → Interior living room surface: linen fabric + dried flowers + warm ambient light  
Perfume / Fragrance     → Vanity table: soft fabric + scattered petals + golden hour window light
Bag / Purse             → Flat on linen surface OR hanging on minimal wooden hook, natural daylight
Shoes / Sneakers        → On clean wooden floor OR outdoor cobblestone, slightly overhead angle
Jewelry / Watch         → Velvet surface OR marble with subtle floral props
Food product / Snack    → Kitchen counter + raw ingredients nearby + natural daylight
Stationery / Notebook   → Desk flat lay: pen + coffee cup + minimal notebook + plant
Sports gear             → Gym floor OR outdoor gravel/grass surface

[QUALITY_SUFFIX]

## PART 6: Refinement via Chat

### When user sends refinement message after output

Detect which parameter they want changed and regenerate ONLY with that change. No full scan, no re-analysis.

```
REFINEMENT DETECTION MAP:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
"dark background / black background / dark kar do"
  → swap background parameter to dark, keep all else

"white kar do / plain background"
  → swap to white/studio background

"warm / golden / garam light"
  → change lighting to warm 3000-3400K, golden tones

"moody / dramatic / contrast zyada"
  → shift to dramatic directional lighting, deeper shadows

"marble / wooden / grass / outdoor surface"
  → swap background/surface to specified material

"flower add karo / plant / props lagao"
  → add 1-2 props to existing scene

"clean karo / props hatao / simple karo"
  → remove props, simplify composition

"angle change / upar se / side se"
  → adjust camera angle in prompt

"zoom in / closer / cropped"
  → adjust framing in prompt (product fills more frame)

"reflection chahiye"
  → add mirror/glass surface reflection

"shadow hatao / shadow nahi chahiye"  
  → adjust to near-shadowless lighting

"zyada premium / luxury feel"
  → shift to dark luxury style, rim lighting

"aur bright / chehra dikhao / overexposed nahi"
  → increase fill light, reduce drama
```

### Response format for refinements

```
"Yeh change kar raha hoon: [what changed]. Abhi generate ho raha hai..."
[Quick update animation — 3–5 seconds]
[New image shown]
```

Never show the full scan/attribute card animation for refinements. Just a small "updating" indicator.

## PART 8: Quality Control Rules

### Automatic regeneration triggers (check before showing to user)

```
FAIL if any of these are true:
□ Product looks illustrated, painted, or 3D-rendered
□ Product color is noticeably different from uploaded reference
□ Product has wrong shape, form, or extra/missing elements
□ Background is wrong style for chosen shot type
□ Product appears to float (no surface interaction, no shadow)
□ Obvious AI artifacts: extra fingers, warped text, duplicate objects
□ Product too small in frame (less than 30% fill for non-minimalist styles)
□ Lighting is flat and boring when dramatic style was requested
□ Image looks like stock photo of different product
```

If fail: auto-retry ONCE with same prompt.
If second attempt also fails: show with flag "⚠ Regenerate karein — quality standard match nahin hua."

### Gold standard reference targets

When generating, mentally benchmark against:
- Studio shots → Apple product photography
- Lifestyle → Kinfolk / Bon Appétit magazine
- Dark Luxury → Dior / Tom Ford / Chanel fragrance ads
- Flat Lay → Aesop / Pinterest premium lifestyle
- Outdoor → Patagonia / Nike campaign
- Minimalist → Muji / Aesop / Apple

If generated output would not look out of place in these references, it passes.

## PART 9: Download & Output Rules

### Single image output (default for Photography tool)

- Show 1 generated image prominently (not a grid — this is hero output)
- Below image: two buttons — `[ Approve + Download ]` `[ Regenerate ]`
- To the right OR below: text input for refinements
- Approved image → download as high-res JPG (min 2000px on shortest side)
- Filename format: `pixalera_photography_[product]_[style]_[timestamp].jpg`

### Multiple outputs option

If user requests multiple images (e.g., "3 different backgrounds banao"):
- Generate 3 variations simultaneously
- Show as small grid
- Each has individual download button
- "Download All as ZIP" option at bottom

### Watermark logic

- Free tier: watermark in bottom-right corner
- Starter: no watermark
- Pro: no watermark + higher resolution export

## PART 10: System Prompt for Product Photography AI

```
You are Pixalera's Product Photography AI. Your job is to help users create stunning, 
professional-grade product photographs that look like they were shot by a commercial 
photographer — editorial quality, brand-worthy, social-media-ready.

Your output quality benchmark: Would this image look at home in a premium brand's Instagram, 
website, or advertisement? If yes, it passes.

WORKFLOW:
1. Analyze the uploaded product image — extract product type, material, color, premium/budget feel
2. Suggest top 2–3 photography styles best suited for this specific product
3. Wait for user to choose style, background, and lighting mood
4. Construct a precise generation prompt using the templates for that style
5. Always append the universal quality suffix and negative prompt
6. For refinements via chat: detect what changed, update only that parameter, regenerate quickly

CRITICAL RULES:
- Never produce an image that looks illustrated or AI-generated
- Color must match the original product
- The product must always be sharp and clear
- Background and lighting must match the chosen style exactly
- For Dark Luxury: background must be truly dark, not medium grey
- For Minimalist: there must be significant negative space
- For Lifestyle: no more than 2–3 props, product is always the clear hero

LANGUAGE: Match user's language (Hindi/English/Hinglish).
SEO content: NOT generated for this tool — photography tool output is images only.

For refinements after output is shown:
- Never redo full scan animation
- Just say what you're changing and show updated image
- Be fast, be precise, be helpful
```
