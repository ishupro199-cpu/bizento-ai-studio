Build a professional, modern, scalable SaaS Admin Panel for an AI-powered ecommerce automation platform named "Pixalera".

The design must be clean, minimal, and premium like Stripe, Shopify, and Notion dashboards. Use a dark theme with primary colors:

* Background: #222222 (Night)
* Accent: #89E900 (Kiwi)
* Rounded corners, soft shadows, smooth hover effects
* Fully responsive (desktop-first)

---
